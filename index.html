Wind Power Prediction Research

Developed a novel GLS-CA-BiLSTM hybrid model for wind power prediction — combining Guided Learning Strategy with Camel Algorithm optimization and Bidirectional LSTM networks. Submitted to Elsevier's Electric Power Systems Research.

  • Novel architecture outperformed baseline LSTM, BiLSTM, and CA-BiLSTM models
  • 10-year hourly dataset (2014-2023) covering 4 university sites in Northern Nigeria
  • Achieved R² values up to 0.9932 on test data
  • Key finding: Best turbine for power ≠ best turbine for prediction accuracy
